// FAQ

Common questions

Do I need electronics experience? ▾
None at all. Module 2 covers every electronics concept from scratch. If you can connect a USB cable, you can start this course.
What hardware do I need? ▾
An Arduino Uno, L298N motor driver, HC-SR04 sensor, SG90 servo, 2WD chassis kit, TT motors, and a battery. Total cost is around ₹1,600 in India — all available on Robu.in or Amazon.
What software do I need? ▾
Just the free Arduino IDE (arduino.cc). No paid software required.
How long does the course take? ▾
At 1 hour/day, most learners complete it in 4 weeks. Learn at your own pace — no deadline.
Will I have a real robot at the end? ▾
Yes! By Module 9 you'll have a fully working autonomous robot that you designed, wired, and programmed yourself.
Is this course free? ▾
100% free. All AlCoders lessons, code, and resources are free. Just start!
