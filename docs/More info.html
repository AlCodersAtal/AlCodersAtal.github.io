Viswadeepthi English Medium School is a well-established institution located in Kattakada, Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala. The school is affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) and offers education from primary to senior secondary levels. It is known for its balanced approach to academics and extracurricular activities, encouraging students to pursue interests beyond the classroom. The school is headed by Fr. John Varghese, who has an impressive background in education, holding qualifications like M.Sc., M.Phil., and Ph.D. He has over 30 years of teaching experience and one year of administrative experience. The school provides a comprehensive education with a focus on both academic excellence and holistic development. Parents and students generally rate the school positively, highlighting the supportive faculty and the school's commitment to student well-being. The infrastructure is also well-regarded, providing a conducive environment for learning.
