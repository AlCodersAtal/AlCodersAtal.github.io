Components
What You Need

All available at any electronics shop or online for under ₹200.

1
Arduino Uno (or Nano)Any 5V Arduino board. Nano is more compact for prototyping.
2
LDR — GL5528 RecommendedResistance: 1kΩ (bright) → 1MΩ (dark). Wide sensing range.
3
LED + 220Ω ResistorAny 5mm LED. Resistor protects it from overcurrent damage.
4
10kΩ ResistorPull-down resistor for the voltage divider with the LDR.
5
Breadboard + Jumper WiresFull or half-size breadboard. Male-to-male dupont wires.
