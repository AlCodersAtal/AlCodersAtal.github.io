Overview

What is AlCoders?

AlCoders is the technology and innovation wing of the Atal Tinkering Lab at Viswadeepthi English Medium School. Guided by our mentor Mr. Praveen Chandran, we foster the spirit of invention through hands-on learning, real-world problem-solving, and futuristic projects that push beyond the textbook.

Atal Tinkering Labs (ATL) are government-funded creative workspaces that give students access to tools like robotics kits, 3D printers, IoT modules, and microcontrollers — everything required to explore, experiment, and engineer.

Purpose

Mission & Vision

At AlCoders, our mission is to ignite a passion for technology and innovation in young minds. We believe the future belongs to those who can understand, create, and shape technology — not merely consume it. We are committed to delivering an ecosystem where students explore STEM through hands-on experiences, real-world problem-solving, and mentorship that encourages bold experimentation.

We aim to break the boundaries of traditional learning by introducing students to robotics, artificial intelligence, machine learning, IoT, and 3D design from an early age. Our goal is to develop not just engineers, but creators, critical thinkers, and ethical innovators.

Vision: To empower every student to be a changemaker through technology, innovation, and collaborative learning — and to lead India's next technological revolution.
