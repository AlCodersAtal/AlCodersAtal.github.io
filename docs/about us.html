What is ATL?

Atal Tinkering Labs (ATL) are creative workspaces in schools that promote STEM-based learning. Students get access to tools like robotics kits, 3D printers, and IoT modules to explore, experiment, and innovate.

What You Learn at AlCoders

  • Arduino and microcontroller programming
  • 3D modeling and printing
  • IoT projects and automation
  • Mobile app development
  • Basic AI and machine learning fundamentals
  • Teamwork, critical thinking, and ethical innovation

Our Mission

At AlCoders, our mission is to ignite a passion for technology and innovation in young minds. We believe that the future belongs to those who can understand, create, and shape technology—not just use it.

That’s why we are committed to providing an ecosystem where students can explore the vast landscape of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) through hands-on experiences and real-world problem-solving.

We aim to break the boundaries of traditional learning by introducing students to cutting-edge domains like robotics, artificial intelligence, machine learning, Internet of Things (IoT), and 3D design from an early age.

Our mission is not just to teach students how to code or build; it’s to develop creators, innovators, and critical thinkers who are equipped to thrive in a technology-driven world. Through workshops, mentorship, and peer collaboration, we help students ask questions, experiment boldly, and build fearlessly.

Our Vision

To empower every student to be a changemaker through technology, innovation, and collaborative learning. We envision a future where students lead India’s next tech revolution.
