Know about ATL

Atal Tinkering Labs (ATL) are dynamic spaces in schools across India where students unleash their creativity and dive into hands-on innovation. Imagine a lab filled with cutting-edge tools like 3D printers, robotics kits, and IoT gadgetsâ€”all designed to turn students' wildest ideas into reality. ATL is all about making learning fun and empowering young minds to think critically and solve real-world problems. With the guidance of industry mentors and the thrill of competitions, these labs foster a culture of innovation that could shape the future. It's where curiosity meets technology, and the next generation of inventors begins their journey.

What Things can I learn from AlCoders?

We teach the students how to build and program robots, how to debug and rectify errors, create 3D designs and print them, and even connect devices through the Internet of Things (IoT). They get hands-on experience with coding, electronics, and cutting-edge technologies like artificial intelligence, all while solving real-world problems through creative projects. These labs emphasize teamwork, ethical thinking, and sustainable innovation, helping students not just to learn but to think critically and inventively about the future.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire and nurture the next generation of innovators and entrepreneurs. We aim to provide a platform where young minds can explore, experiment, and excel in the fields of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM). Through hands-on learning and mentorship, we strive to cultivate a culture of creativity, critical thinking, and problem-solving.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a world where every student has the opportunity to realize their full potential and contribute to the advancement of society. We envision a future where innovation and technology are accessible to all, and where young people are empowered to make a positive impact on the world.
